Blog SECTION

  • This is the blog section. It lists all posts placed under '/content/blog/'
  • By making that '/blog' subdirectory under '/content' we've created a 'section' called 'blog'
  • This is the template for that section. It lives at: '/skeletontheme/layouts/section/blog.html'
